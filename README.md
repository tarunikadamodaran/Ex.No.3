## INTRODUCTION
Prompt Engineering is the process of designing effective instructions or questions to guide AI systems in generating accurate and meaningful responses. Different prompting techniques are used depending on the desired output format and complexity of the task.

This experiment demonstrates four common prompting techniques:

Straightforward Prompting
Tabular Format Prompting
Preceding Question Prompting
Missing Word Prompting

These prompting methods help improve communication between users and AI systems


## 1. STRAIGHTFORWARD PROMPTS
Definition:
Straightforward prompting directly asks the AI a simple question or instruction without additional context.

Example 1
Prompt:  Define Artificial Intelligence in one sentence.
Response:  Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines and computer systems.

Example 2
Prompt:  List three advantages of cloud computing.
Response:
Scalability
Cost Efficiency
Remote Accessibility

# Features
Simple and direct
Easy to understand
Quick response generation

## 3. PRECEDING QUESTION PROMPTING
Definition:
Preceding Question Prompting provides a guiding question before asking the main question. This improves contextual understanding.

Example 1
Prompt: Why is cybersecurity important? Explain how firewalls protect computer networks.
Response:
Cybersecurity is important because it protects systems, networks, and data from cyber attacks and unauthorized access. Firewalls protect computer networks by monitoring and filtering incoming and outgoing traffic based on security rules.

Example 2
Prompt: Why are renewable energy sources important? Explain the working of solar panels.
Response:
Renewable energy sources are important because they reduce pollution and dependency on fossil fuels. Solar panels work by converting sunlight into electrical energy using photovoltaic cells.

# Characteristics
Provides context
Improves AI understanding
Produces detailed responses

## 4. MISSING WORD PROMPTING
Definition:
Missing Word Prompting asks the AI to fill in the blank with the correct word or phrase.

Example 1
Prompt: The CPU is known as the ____ of the computer.
Response:
brain
Example 2
Prompt:
In Machine Learning, data is used to train ____.
Response:
models

# Features
Useful for quizzes and assessments
Improves recall ability
Simple and interactive
